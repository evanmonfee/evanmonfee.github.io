Two heads are better than one!

Being a Coder is liberating indeed! It makes you feel you can devise solutions to every problem at hand. Once, after a long day in Auroville, Pondicherry, my sister and I sat down to settle up the expenses between the two of us. Recollection turned gruelling as we had been postponing the calculations for quite some time. I immediately wanted to design an algorithm that could take care of the split up based on data fed over a period of time. So my sister, an accountant by qualification, and I sat down to work, and in a while we had a neat java program that could take inputs from the console and perform the heavy lifting. From there it turned into an online mobile app, which now has 300+ downloads and 4.9 rating on Google Play
